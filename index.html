ABOUT US
Gaming Club, BITS Pilani is a community of students who have a shared interest in gaming. We are responsible for hosting various gaming events in the campus. We host two major events, IGNITION during the BITS Open Sports Meet (BOSM) and ARMAGEDDON during APOGEE, the tech fest of BITS Pilani. These events have a massive participation pool of around 1.5k-2k Gaming Enthusiast from BITS Pilani and other colleges, from all over the country. We are dedicated to “play games”, promote the culture of gaming in the campus and create a competitive environment. We play all types of games on all platforms like PC, Mobile, Consoles. We majorly host games like Rainbow 6 Siege, CS: GO, Valorant, FIFA, COD, PUBG Mobile, COD Mobile etc.
